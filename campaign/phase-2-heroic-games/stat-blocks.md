## Mimic (MM p.220, No Modifications)

> *Medium monstrosity (shapechanger), neutral*

Used for: Labyrinth of Trials — Event 1, Mimic Hall.

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **AC** | 12 (natural armor) |
| **HP** | 58 (9d8 + 18) |
| **Speed** | 15 ft. |
| **CR** | 2 (450 XP) |

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 17 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 15 (+2) | 5 (−3) | 13 (+1) | 8 (−1) |

**Skills:** Stealth +5
**Damage Immunities:** Acid
**Condition Immunities:** Prone
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11

**Shapechanger.** The mimic can use its action to polymorph into an object or
back into its true, amorphous form. Its statistics are the same in each form.
Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn't transformed. It reverts to its
true form if it dies.

**Adhesive (Object Form Only).** The mimic adheres to anything that touches it.
A Huge or smaller creature adhered to the mimic is also grappled by it (escape
DC 13). Ability checks made to escape this grapple have disadvantage.

**False Appearance (Object Form Only).** While the mimic remains motionless, it
is indistinguishable from an ordinary object.

**Grappler.** The mimic has advantage on attack rolls against any creature
grappled by it.

**ACTIONS**

- **Pseudopod.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
  *Hit:* 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage. If the mimic is in object form, the
  target is subjected to its Adhesive trait.

- **Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
  *Hit:* 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage plus 4 (1d8) acid damage.

## Gynosphinx (Reduced — CR 8 Variant)

> *Large monstrosity, lawful neutral*

Used for: Labyrinth of Trials — Event 1, Riddle Sphinx.
This is a reduced stat block for a CR 8 challenge. Only fights if riddles
are answered incorrectly.

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **AC** | 17 (natural armor) |
| **HP** | 105 (14d10 + 28) |
| **Speed** | 40 ft., fly 60 ft. |
| **CR** | 8 (3,900 XP) |

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 18 (+4) | 15 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 18 (+4) | 18 (+4) | 18 (+4) |

**Skills:** Arcana +8, History +8, Perception +8, Religion +8
**Damage Resistances:** Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from nonmagical
attacks
**Damage Immunities:** Psychic
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Frightened
**Senses:** Truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 18
**Languages:** Common, Sphinx

**Inscrutable.** The sphinx is immune to any effect that would sense its
emotions or read its thoughts, as well as any divination spell that it refuses.
Wisdom (Insight) checks made to ascertain the sphinx's intentions or sincerity
have disadvantage.

**Magic Weapons.** The sphinx's weapon attacks are magical.

**Spellcasting (Reduced).** The sphinx is a 9th-level spellcaster. Its
spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell
attacks). It has the following spells prepared:

- Cantrips: *Sacred Flame*, *Mage Hand*
- 1st level (4 slots): *Detect Magic*, *Shield*
- 2nd level (3 slots): *Hold Person*, *Suggestion*
- 3rd level (3 slots): *Counterspell*, *Dispel Magic*
- 4th level (2 slots): *Banishment*

**ACTIONS**

- **Multiattack.** The sphinx makes two claw attacks.

- **Claw.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
  *Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) slashing damage.

**RIDDLES (Use 3 — Wrong Answer = Combat)**

1. *"I have cities, but no houses. I have mountains, but no trees. I have
   water, but no fish."* **Answer: A map.**

2. *"The more you take, the more you leave behind."* **Answer: Footsteps.**

3. *"I can be cracked, made, told, and played."* **Answer: A joke.**

**Behavior:** The sphinx fights for 3 rounds maximum, then flies to the
ceiling and lets them pass (it was testing resolve, not trying to kill).

## Gilded Sentinel (Custom — Shield Guardian Variant)

> *Large construct, unaligned*

Used for: Adventure 4 (Grayhold Ruins). Valerius's mid-tier enforcer.

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **AC** | 18 (natural armor) |
| **HP** | 142 (15d10 + 60) |
| **Speed** | 30 ft. |
| **CR** | 7 (2,900 XP) |

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 18 (+4) | 8 (−1) | 18 (+4) | 7 (−2) | 10 (+0) | 3 (−4) |

**Damage Immunities:** Poison
**Damage Vulnerability:** Thunder (the glass shatters)
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Exhaustion, Frightened, Paralyzed,
Poisoned
**Senses:** Blindsight 10 ft., Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
**Languages:** Understands commands given by Valerius but can't speak

**Bound.** The sentinel is magically bound to Valerius's will. It follows
the last command given.

**Regeneration.** The sentinel regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn
if it has at least 1 hit point.

**Spell Storing.** The sentinel can store one spell of 4th level or lower.
Currently stores: *Misty Step* (1 use).

**ACTIONS**

- **Multiattack.** The sentinel makes two fist attacks.
- **Fist.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
  *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage.
- **Misty Step (1/day, Stored Spell).** The sentinel teleports up to 30 ft.
  to an unoccupied space it can see. (Used to close distance or bypass
  barricades.)

**REACTIONS**

- **Shield.** When a creature it can see within 5 feet of it is the target of
  an attack, the sentinel can impose disadvantage on the attack roll (it
  interposes its body). If guarding the Anchor, it will do this for the
  Anchor's pedestal.

**On Death:** Shatters into golden glass shards. A recording of Valerius's
voice plays: *"Return what was taken."*

**DM Behavior:**
- The Sentinel is dormant in the vault until the Anchor is touched OR Quill is
  threatened by violence.
- It prioritizes recovering the Anchor. If the players have it, it pursues
  them. If Quill has it, it attacks Quill.
- It uses Misty Step to bypass barricades or close distance on fleeing targets.
- **Exploit the thunder vulnerability** if the players have *Shatter* or
  *Thunderwave*. Reward smart play.

- **AC 20** (plate + shield instead of splint)

  (concentration).
- **Golden Guard Coordination:** When a Golden Guard hits a creature, the
  next Golden Guard to attack that creature before the start of the first
  guard's next turn has advantage.
