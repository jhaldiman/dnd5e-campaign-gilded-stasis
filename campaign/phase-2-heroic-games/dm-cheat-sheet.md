## Quick Rules Reminders

| Rule | Quick Reference |
| --- | --- |
| **Airship combat** | Ship HP = 30, AC 13. Characters can fall overboard (Dex DC 14 to grab railing). Falling from airship: 10d6 unless Feather Fall. |
| **Non-lethal KO (Mock Siege)** | Melee attacks can knock to 0 without killing. Ranged CANNOT unless DM rules otherwise. Mock Siege is all non-lethal. |
| **Infiltration/Stealth** | Group Stealth = everyone rolls, half must succeed. Armor disadvantage applies. |
| **Changeling disguise (Sable)** | Sable can change appearance at will. No check required. Players can only see through it with Truesight or if Sable drops the disguise. |
| **Dispel Magic vs. Pod** | Requires 7th-level slot OR ability check DC 17 (for lower slots). |
| **Concentration** | Max 1 concentration spell. Damaged = CON save DC 10 or half damage (whichever higher). |
| **Milestone leveling** | Level 7 after Labyrinth, 8 after Regatta + investigation, 9 after Mock Siege + investigation, 10 after Adventure 4, 11 after Hall of Statues. |
