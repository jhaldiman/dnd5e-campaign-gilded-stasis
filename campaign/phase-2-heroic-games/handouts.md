## Handout #4 — Valerius's Quest Briefing

*Hand out at the start of Adventure 4.*

```
╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               CONFIDENTIAL — EYES ONLY                    ║
║                                                           ║
║   TO:      [Player Party Name]                            ║
║   FROM:    Office of the Archduke                         ║
║   RE:      Recovery of Stolen Artifact                    ║
║                                                           ║
║   A group of radical scholars calling themselves the      ║
║   "Order of Ashen Knowledge" has stolen an artifact of    ║
║   significant historical and strategic importance from    ║
║   the Solaran National Archives.                          ║
║                                                           ║
║   The artifact — a crystalline orb known as the           ║
║   "Meridian Stone" — is believed to be an Anchor of       ║
║   pre-Tri-Kingdom origin. In the wrong hands, it          ║
║   could destabilize regional ley-lines and threaten       ║
║   the safety of thousands.                                ║
║                                                           ║
║   Intelligence places the stolen artifact at the          ║
║   Ruins of Grayhold, a fortress 3 days northeast of      ║
║   Aurelius. The scholars are believed to be lightly       ║
║   armed but may have hired sellswords.                    ║
║                                                           ║
║   Your mission: recover the artifact and return it to     ║
║   Solhaven Palace. Herald Elara Dawnmantle will           ║
║   accompany you as liaison and support.                   ║
║                                                           ║
║   The Archduke trusts you with this above all others.     ║
║                                                           ║
║   — V.T.                                                  ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

**DM Note:** Every claim in this briefing is technically true but
misleading. The "Order of Ashen Knowledge" is the Silver Crows. The
"Meridian Stone" is the Anchor of Death. They didn't steal it — they've
been protecting it. When the players realize this, re-read the handout.
It should feel different the second time.

skin crawl.
