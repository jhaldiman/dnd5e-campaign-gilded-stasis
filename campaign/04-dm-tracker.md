## Pre-Session Checklist

Before every session, answer these four questions:

1. **What is Valerius doing right now?**
   _(Refer to the BBEG Action Tracker in 01-bbeg-and-villains.md)_

2. **How does that touch the players this session?**
   _(A rumor? An NPC acting strangely? A direct encounter?)_

3. **What do the players WANT to do?**
   _(Based on last session's cliffhanger or stated plans.)_

4. **What's the "hell yeah" moment?**
   _(One exciting set-piece, dramatic reveal, or heroic beat per session.)_

## Countdown Clock — The Festival of Eternal Light

Once the players enter Act III, start a **countdown clock**. The Festival
occurs on a fixed date. The players have a limited number of "actions" (each
adventure = 1 action, roughly 1-2 weeks of in-game time).

| Ticks Remaining | World State |
| --- | --- |
| **5** | Things seem normal. Valerius publicly announces the Festival date. |
| **4** | Shadows grow faint. Sundials run slow across the Tri-Kingdoms. |
| **3** | The wind dies for a full day. Crops stop growing. Animals are restless. |
| **2** | Time hiccups. People repeat sentences. Déjà vu is constant. |
| **1** | The sun visibly slows. Dawn takes 3 hours. Night barely comes. |
| **0** | The Festival begins. The Citadel manifests. The ritual starts. |

Each Anchor the players destroy ticks the clock backward by 1. Each session
they spend *not* addressing an Anchor ticks it forward by 1. This creates
natural tension without being a strict timer.
