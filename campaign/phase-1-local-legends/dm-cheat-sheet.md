## Quick Rules Reminders

| Rule | Quick Reference |
| --- | --- |
| **Non-lethal KO** | Melee attack can knock to 0 HP without killing (player's choice) |
| **Surprise** | Surprised creatures can't move or take actions on first turn, no reactions until turn ends |
| **Cover** | Half cover: +2 AC/Dex saves. Three-quarters: +5 AC/Dex saves |
| **Falling** | 1d6 per 10 ft., max 20d6 |
| **Suffocating** | 1 + CON min (min 30 sec), then CON rounds before dropping to 0 |
| **Grapple** | Athletics vs. Athletics/Acrobatics. Escape same. Speed = 0 for grappled |
| **Object Interaction** | 1 free per turn (open door, draw weapon). Second = action |
| **Milestone leveling** | Level 4 after Adventure 1B, Level 5 after Adventure 3 |
