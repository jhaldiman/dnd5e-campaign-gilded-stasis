# Phase 1 — Player Handouts

> Print these out, cut them apart, and hand them to your players at the
> appropriate moment. Physical handouts make the world feel real.

---

## Handout 5: Corwin's Apology Note to the Leopards

*Hand to players when they encounter the robbed Leopards on the road
(Adventure 3, Encounter 2). Gareth shoves this at them angrily.*

---

> My Dear Lucky Leopards,
>
> Please accept my sincerest apologies for the inconvenience of
> today's toll collection. I assure you, your valuables will be
> put to excellent use (primarily food, shelter, and tablecloths).
>
> I have taken the liberty of leaving you a basket of fresh
> apricots as compensation. They are in season and, I am told,
> quite good for morale.
>
> Regarding the greatsword: I will keep it safe. It is a fine
> weapon, if somewhat optimistically named.
>
> Should you wish to discuss the matter further, I am available
> most afternoons in the pass. Please send word ahead — I prefer
> to set a proper table for guests.
>
> With warm regards and genuine respect,
>
> *Sir Corwin Ashford*
> *Prince of the Pass*
>
> *P.S. — Your wizard's spellbook makes for surprisingly*
> *entertaining reading. The margin notes are inspired.*

## Handout 6: The Survey Permit

*Hand to players if they ask Foreman Harwick for proof of authority
(Adventure 2, Encounter 3). Harwick produces it proudly.*

---

> ### OFFICIAL SURVEY PERMIT
> ### Tri-Kingdoms Infrastructure Bureau
>
> **Project:** Silver-Leaf Trade Route Improvement Initiative
> **Authorized by:** The Office of Archduke Valerius Thorne
> **Permit No.:** TK-IR-4471-SL
>
> The bearer of this permit is authorized to conduct
> geographical and arcane survey operations in the
> designated region of the **Whispering Woods** (Western
> Silver-Leaf Province, Grid Ref. 14-W through 22-W).
>
> Survey activities may include:
> - Terrain assessment and mapping
> - Soil and mineral sampling
> - **Ley-line conductivity measurement** ← *(This line is
>   in smaller print, easy to miss. DC 14 Perception to
>   notice on first read.)*
> - Vegetation clearance as needed
>
> This permit supersedes local ordinances. All regional
> authorities are required to provide full cooperation.
>
> *Signed,*
> *Lucretia Voss*
> *Director, Preservation Society*
> *On behalf of Archduke Valerius Thorne*
>
> *(Embossed golden seal of the Archduke's office)*

**DM Note:** If a player notices the "Ley-line conductivity measurement"
line, reward them — it's the first hard clue that the Archduke's interest
in this area is more than just road-building. The signature of Lucretia
Voss is also a seed for Act II.

> *(Fill in 1–2 of that character's achievements:*

**DM Tip:** Personalizing the "Recognized Deeds" line for each player makes
this feel special. Reference specific moments from the sessions — the more
specific, the better. If one player disabled a construct non-lethally, write
"Masterful Disabling of Arcane Constructs." If one negotiated with Corwin,
write "Diplomatic Resolution of the Emerald Pass Crisis." Valerius is
watching. He knows everything.

## Handout 9: Silver Signet Ring Sketch

*Hand to the player who finds the ring in the Wine-Cellar tomb (Adventure 1B,
Encounter 5). A simple drawing is enough.*

---

> **ITEM: Silver Signet Ring**
>
> A heavy silver ring, tarnished with age. The face bears an
> engraved motif: a single leaf with seven veins, enclosed in
> a circle. The interior band is inscribed with letters too
> worn to read — only "...ren of the Sil..." is legible.
>
> *(The leaf motif is distinctive. It doesn't match any current
> noble house in the Tri-Kingdoms. It must be very old.)*
>
> **Value:** ~25 gp as jewelry. Possibly more to the right
> collector or historian.

**DM Note:** This leaf motif is the symbol of the **Anchor of Change**. The
players will see it again on the Anchor itself in Act II — and it should
trigger an "oh!" moment at the table. Don't explain this now. Just let the
seed sit.

## Handout 10: Oakhaven Region Map (Player Version)

*Hand to players at Session 1 or pin to the table for reference. This
version contains only what the characters would reasonably know.*

---

```
                                    N
                                    ↑
                              EMERALD PASS
                            (mountain road)
                                  |
                                  |   ← 30 miles
                                  |
        WHISPERING             OAKHAVEN
          WOODS          ★ Crossroads Inn
        (Verdaine         ★ Town Hall
         border)          ★ Cogsworth's Workshop
            \              ★ Shrine of Dawnlight
             \              |
              \             |  ← Clearbrook River →  (to Ferromar)
               \            |
                \     DELACROIX VINEYARD
                 \      (2 miles south)
                  \         |
                   \        |   ← Trade Road
                    \       |
                  (to Verdaine)    (to Aurelius — ~150 miles)
```

> **Key:**
> - ★ = Notable building in Oakhaven
> - The Clearbrook River runs east toward Ferromar's coast.
> - Emerald Pass is a 1.5-day walk north through the Ashenmere Hills.
> - The Whispering Woods mark the unofficial border with Verdaine.
> - The main Trade Road runs south toward the Aurelius highway.
