### Terrain Rules — Underwater Combat
| Rule | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Underwater movement | All movement is difficult terrain unless you have a swim speed |
| Melee attacks | Disadvantage unless using dagger, javelin, shortsword, spear, or trident |
| Ranged attacks | Auto-miss beyond normal range. Disadvantage on attacks within normal range (except crossbows) |
| Fire spells | Do not function underwater |
| Lightning spells | 5-ft radius splash. All creatures within 5 ft of target also take damage |
| Visibility | Dim light beyond 30 ft. Darkvision reduced by half |

### Clock Mechanic
| Initiative | Event |
|------------|-------|
| Count 20 | The minute hand sweeps ONE sector (covering 2 hour-positions). All creatures standing in the swept sector: DC 16 Constitution save or gain 1 Exhaustion (time distortion) |
| Count 10 | The clock chimes. All creatures on the clock face make DC 13 Wisdom save or are Frightened until the end of their next turn |

| Feature | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Clock face | Difficult terrain (gears and grooves) |
| Off the clock | Normal floor. Safe from the sweep mechanic |
| Access panel | Perception DC 15 to find at the "6 o'clock" position. Thieves' Tools DC 17 to jam, or 50+ damage to smash |
| Jamming the clock | Stops the sweep and chime. Animated Armors deactivate. Sentinels remain |

### Anchor Aura Effects
| Anchor | Aura (20 ft) | Destroying It |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| Anchor of Time | Speed halved, cannot take reactions | AC 20, 100 HP (radiant/force only), regenerates 20 HP/round. Or *Dispel Magic* DC 18 |
| Anchor of Life | Cannot regain HP. Healing spells deal damage instead | AC 20, 100 HP (radiant/force only), regenerates 20 HP/round. Or *Dispel Magic* DC 18 |

### Elara (If Not Turned)
- She stands between the party and the Anchors, weeping
- She fights reluctantly: pulls punches (half damage on melee), heals downed PCs on her turn
- Can still be turned mid-fight: Persuasion DC 22 (disadvantage in combat)
- If turned here, she destroys one Anchor herself (dramatic moment)

### Sanctuary Effect
- While at least one Anchor is active, Valerius cannot be targeted or damaged (Sanctuary, no save)
- Both Anchors must be destroyed before entering Room 4

### Boss Arena Rules
| Feature | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Ritual Circle | Creatures inside take 2d6 radiant damage at start of their turn (heroic essence discharge). Valerius is immune |
| Valerius hovers at 15 ft | Melee requires reach weapons, flight, or climbing a pillar (Athletics DC 12, then 5-ft jump to reach him) |
| Pillars (P) | Half cover. 20 ft tall. Can be climbed. A creature atop a pillar is within melee range of Valerius |
| No ceiling | Fly spells work. No upper boundary. The "sky" is a golden dome that looks infinite |
| No shadows | Spells that require shadows (Shadow Blade, etc.) do not function |

### Lair Actions (Initiative Count 20)
| d3 | Lair Action |
|----|-------------|
| 1 | **Gravity Inversion:** One 20-ft square. DC 16 Dex save or "fall" 30 ft up (3d6 bludgeoning) then 30 ft down (3d6 bludgeoning) |
| 2 | **Time Pool:** 15-ft radius. DC 16 Con save or 4d8 necrotic + 1 Exhaustion (rapid aging) |
| 3 | **Frozen Moment:** One creature. DC 17 Wis save or Paralyzed until end of their next turn |

### Phase Transition
- At 50% HP, Valerius drops to the ground (no longer hovering)
- The ritual circle flares — its damage increases to 4d6 radiant
- Valerius gains Legendary Actions (see stat blocks)
- The frozen sun overhead begins to **pulse** — visual countdown
