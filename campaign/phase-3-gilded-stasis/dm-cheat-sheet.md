## Quick Rules Reminders

| Rule | Reference |
|------|-----------|
| Exhaustion (5e 2014) | 1: Disadvantage on ability checks. 2: Speed halved. 3: Disadvantage on attacks/saves. 4: HP max halved. 5: Speed 0. 6: Death. |
| Underwater combat | Melee disadvantage (except listed weapons). Ranged auto-miss beyond normal. No fire spells. Lightning splashes 5 ft. |
| Sanctuary | Target can't be attacked. Ends if the protected creature attacks or casts hostile spell |
| Skill Challenge | 5 successes before 3 failures. Each PC can attempt one skill per round. |
| Legendary Resistance | After learning of it, players may try to "burn" resistances with lower-priority spells before the big ones |
| Paralyzed | Auto-fail Str/Dex saves. Attacks have advantage. Hits within 5 ft. are auto-crits |
| Hover | Not affected by falling when speed becomes 0 (unlike regular flight) |
