### Duplicate King Thalric (Archmage Variant)

**Custom — CR 12 | 8,400 XP**

> *A perfect copy of King Thalric Greenbough, crafted from chronomantic energy
> and golden sand. It looks, sounds, and even smells like the real king — but
> its eyes never blink, and its smile never changes.*

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **AC** | 15 (natural armor — temporal distortion field) |
| **HP** | 150 (20d8 + 60) |
| **Speed** | 30 ft. |
| **STR** | 10 (+0) |
| **DEX** | 14 (+2) |
| **CON** | 16 (+3) |
| **INT** | 20 (+5) |
| **WIS** | 15 (+2) |
| **CHA** | 18 (+4) |

**Saving Throws:** Int +9, Wis +6, Cha +8
**Skills:** Arcana +9, Deception +8, History +9, Insight +6
**Damage Resistances:** Force, Psychic
**Damage Immunities:** Poison
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Frightened, Poisoned
**Senses:** Truesight 30 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages:** Common, Elvish, Sylvan, Draconic

**Magic Resistance.** Advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.

**Temporal Shell.** When the Duplicate drops to 0 HP, it does not die immediately. Instead, it freezes in place for 1 round, then dissolves into golden sand. During this round, it is incapacitated and immune to all damage.

**Vulnerability: Dispel Magic.** *Dispel Magic* cast at 5th level or higher (DC 17) against the Duplicate causes its face to flicker. At 7th level or higher, the disguise drops entirely and it attacks.

#### Actions

**Multiattack.** The Duplicate makes two Chronal Bolt attacks.

**Chronal Bolt.** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +9 to hit, range 120 ft., one target.
*Hit:* 22 (4d8 + 4) force damage. The target's speed is reduced by 10 ft.
until the end of its next turn.

**Slow (At Will).** The Duplicate casts *Slow* (reflavored as temporal
distortion). Save DC 17. Concentration.

**Time Stop (1/Day).** The Duplicate casts *Time Stop*. It gets 1d4 + 1 turns.

#### Reactions

**Temporal Flicker.** When hit by an attack, the Duplicate can use its reaction
to impose disadvantage on the attack roll (it briefly phases out of time). It
can use this reaction 3 times per day.

#### On Death
> *The Duplicate's features freeze — that same smile, locked in place one final
> time — and then it crumbles. Golden sand pours from its form like an hourglass
> emptying. Where it stood: nothing but a faint shimmer and the scent of old
> parchment.*

**Custom | 8,400 XP**

> into every surface. The crystal eye pulses with a deeper, steadier light.*

That creature must succeed on a DC 16 Charisma saving throw or be teleported
to an unoccupied space the Sentinel can see within 60 ft. If this places the
creature in water, difficult terrain, or within 5 ft. of a hazard, the
creature takes 10 (3d6) force damage.

#### On Death
> *The Sentinel shudders, its geometric patterns flashing white, then cracks
> down the center. As it splits apart, the space around it hiccups — furniture
> shifts three inches, water surges against the walls, and for one disorienting
> second, the room feels like it's inside out.*

### Time Echo (Shadow Variant)

**Based on Shadow — MM p. 269 | CR 1/2 | 100 XP**

*Reskinned as golden temporal copies of the player characters.*

**Modifications:**
- Appearance: A shimmering golden silhouette of a specific PC
- **Signature Attack:** Instead of Strength Drain, the Echo uses the
  signature weapon or cantrip of the PC it mirrors:
  - Fighter Echo: Longsword — +4 to hit, 1d8 + 2 slashing
  - Wizard Echo: Fire Bolt — +4 to hit, 2d10 fire (range 120 ft.)
  - Rogue Echo: Shortsword — +4 to hit, 1d6 + 2 piercing + 1d6 sneak (if advantage)
  - Cleric Echo: Mace + Sacred Flame — +3 to hit, 1d6 + 1 bludgeoning; or DC 12 Dex save, 2d8 radiant
- Adjust to match your party's actual classes
- HP: 20 each (deliberately fragile)
- **Mirror Link:** If the corresponding mirror is shattered (AC 13, 10 HP), the Echo is instantly destroyed

**Alternative (More Prep):** Use the players' actual character sheets from
levels 5–7 with reduced HP (half normal). This is more work but *extremely*
memorable.

## Archduke Valerius Thorne — Final Boss

**Custom — CR 18 | 20,000 XP**

> *Silver-streaked dark hair, immaculate cream-and-gold robes. He hovers above
> the ritual circle, surrounded by a maelstrom of swirling golden essence —
> captured dreams and stolen courage. He looks down at you. He looks sad.*

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **AC** | 18 (temporal distortion field) |
| **HP** | 250 (27d8 + 135) |
| **Speed** | 30 ft., fly 60 ft. (hover) |
| **STR** | 10 (+0) |
| **DEX** | 16 (+3) |
| **CON** | 20 (+5) |
| **INT** | 22 (+6) |
| **WIS** | 16 (+3) |
| **CHA** | 20 (+5) |

**Saving Throws:** Int +12, Wis +9, Cha +11, Con +11
**Skills:** Arcana +12, History +12, Insight +9, Persuasion +11
**Damage Resistances:** Force, Radiant
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Frightened
**Senses:** Truesight 60 ft., passive Perception 13
**Languages:** Common, Celestial, Draconic, Elvish, Sylvan

**Legendary Resistance (3/Day).** If Valerius fails a saving throw, he can

he drops below 50% HP.

**Sanctuary (Anchor-Linked).** While at least one Anchor of Reality remains
active in Room 3, Valerius cannot be targeted by attacks or spells (as the
*Sanctuary* spell, no save). Destroy the Anchors first.

### Phase 1 — The Philanthropist (100% to 50% HP)

*Valerius fights with restraint. He's still trying to convince the party.*

#### Actions

**Multiattack.** Valerius makes three Chronal Bolt attacks, or two Chronal
Bolt attacks and casts one spell.

**Chronal Bolt.** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +12 to hit, range 120 ft., one target.
*Hit:* 19 (3d8 + 6) force damage.

**Spellcasting.** Valerius is a 17th-level spellcaster. Spell save DC 20,
+12 to hit.

Phase 1 preferred spells (restrained):
- *Wall of Force* — separates party members for "private conversations"
- *Slow* — temporal distortion, save DC 20
- *Hold Person* (upcast to 5th level, 3 targets) — save DC 20
- *Counterspell* (at 7th level) — he shuts down magic with a disappointed sigh
- *Shield* — reaction, +5 AC until start of next turn

**Between Turns (Monologue):** Valerius talks during Phase 1. He does not
waste actions on it — he simply speaks while fighting, as if the combat is an
inconvenience.

### Phase 2 — The Tyrant (50% to 0% HP)

*The mask drops. His voice hardens. The temporal distortion field fails.
He stops hovering (drops to ground level). He gains Legendary Actions.*

#### Phase Transition

When Valerius drops below 125 HP:
- Read the Phase 2 narration (see read-aloud.md)
- He drops to the ground. Temporal Distortion Field ends
- The ritual circle flares: damage increases from 2d6 to 4d6 radiant
- He gains Legendary Actions and the following additional spells:
  - *Disintegrate* (6th level) — DC 20 Dex save, 10d6 + 40 force
  - *Chain Lightning* (6th level) — DC 20 Dex save, 10d8 lightning
  - *Meteor Swarm* (9th level, 1/day) — if the party groups up, Valerius uses
    this as a desperation move. DC 20 Dex save, 40d6 fire/bludgeoning
  - *Globe of Invulnerability* (6th level, 1/day) — last resort defense

#### Legendary Actions (3/Round, Phase 2 Only)

**Temporal Rewind (1 Action).** Valerius negates the last instance of damage
dealt to him, restoring up to 30 HP. Usable 3/day total (not per round). A
sound like a clock running backward accompanies this.

**Essence Burst (2 Actions).** 60-ft. cone originating from Valerius. Each
creature in the cone must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw, taking
36 (8d8) radiant damage on a failed save, or half on a success. The cone
is visible as a wave of golden light filled with ghostly faces.

**Summon Time Echo (3 Actions, 2/Day).** Valerius tears open a temporal rift
and summons a Time Echo of a previous boss the players defeated. The Echo
appears within 30 ft. of Valerius and acts on its own initiative.

| d4 | Echo Summoned | HP | Key Ability |
|----|---------------|-----|-------------|
| 1 | Wight (Phase 1) | 30 | Life Drain (necrotic, max HP reduction) |
| 2 | Bandit Prince at double power | 60 | 2 attacks, Riposte, Parry |
| 3 | Gilded Sentinel | 70 | Slam, Regeneration 10 |
| 4 | Duplicate King | 75 | Chronal Bolt, Slow |

Time Echoes have half their original HP (shown above), last 3 rounds, and
dissolve into golden sand when destroyed or when their time expires.

### Death and the Final Choice

When Valerius drops to 0 HP, the ritual shatters. He does not die immediately —
he falls to his knees, mortal and broken. He is incapacitated but conscious.

**The ritual's destruction triggers:**
- Stored heroic essence erupts as a wave of golden light across the city
- All stasis pods in the Hall of Statues shatter — champions awaken
- The sun lurches back into motion. Shadows return. The world breathes

The players choose his fate:

| Choice | Consequence |
|--------|-------------|
| **Kill him** | He dies believing he was right. Clean ending. |
| **Spare / Imprison** | Trial by the Tri-Kingdoms. Imprisoned. His ideology lingers (sequel hook). |
| **Use the Stasis on him** | Freeze him in the Citadel forever. Poetic justice. Morally gray. |
