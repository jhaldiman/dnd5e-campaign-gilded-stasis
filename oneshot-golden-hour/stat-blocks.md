## Arena Chimera Spawn

**Custom | CR 3 | 700 XP**

> *A juvenile chimera, bred and conditioned for arena combat. Three
> heads — lion, goat, dragon — all slightly too small for its body, like
> an oversized puppy. The arena handlers have fitted it with a jeweled
> harness that deactivates it if the fight goes wrong.*

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **AC** | 14 (natural armor) |
| **HP** | 52 (7d10 + 14) |
| **Speed** | 30 ft. |
| **STR** | 16 (+3) |
| **DEX** | 11 (+0) |
| **CON** | 14 (+2) |
| **INT** | 3 (−4) |
| **WIS** | 14 (+2) |
| **CHA** | 6 (−2) |

**Skills:** Perception +4
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 14
**Languages:** — (understands handler signals)

**ACTIONS**

- **Bite (Lion Head).** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft.,
  one target. *Hit:* 8 (1d10 + 3) piercing damage.

- **Headbutt (Goat Head).** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach
  5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage. If the
  Spawn moved at least 15 ft. straight toward the target, the target
  must succeed on a DC 13 Strength save or be knocked prone.

- **Fire Spit (Dragon Head, Recharge 6).** 10-ft. cone. Each creature
  in the cone must make a DC 12 Dex save, taking 10 (3d6) fire damage
  on a failed save, or half on a success.

**BEHAVIOR**

These are arena animals. They fight aggressively but can be redirected:
- A character who succeeds on a DC 14 Animal Handling check as an
  action can cause a Spawn to disengage from them for 1 round.
- If reduced to 0 HP, the harness glows and the Spawn falls asleep
  rather than dying.

## Golem of the Seventh Star

**Custom | CR 10 | 5,900 XP**

> *A 12-ft. golden construct in the shape of an armored knight. Its
> chest bears a seven-pointed star that blazes with white light. It
> moves with fluid grace — more dancer than machine. This is the
> marquee attraction of the Gauntlet.*

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **AC** | 18 (natural armor) |
| **HP** | 157 (15d12 + 60) |
| **Speed** | 30 ft. |
| **STR** | 22 (+6) |
| **DEX** | 10 (+0) |
| **CON** | 18 (+4) |
| **INT** | 8 (−1) |
| **WIS** | 12 (+1) |
| **CHA** | 5 (−3) |

**Saving Throws:** Str +10, Con +8
**Damage Immunities:** Poison, Psychic
**Damage Resistances:** Bludgeoning, Piercing, Slashing from
Nonmagical Attacks
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Exhaustion, Frightened, Paralyzed,
Petrified, Poisoned
**Senses:** Darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 11

**Immutable Form.** Immune to form-altering effects.

**Arena Protocol.** Fights honorably. Does not attack unconscious
creatures or fleeing opponents.

**Dramatic Weakness.** At half HP (78 or below), the seven-pointed
star on the Golem's chest cracks and glows red. A DC 13 Arcana or
Perception check (free action) identifies the weak point. Attacks
targeting the star (declared before the roll, no penalty) deal
**double damage.**

**ACTIONS**

- **Multiattack.** Three attacks: two greatsword strikes and one
  shield bash.

- **Golden Greatsword.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach
  10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 19 (2d12 + 6) slashing damage.

- **Shield Bash.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 5 ft.,
  one target. *Hit:* 13 (2d6 + 6) bludgeoning damage, and the target
  must succeed on a DC 18 Strength save or be pushed 10 ft. and
  knocked prone.

- **Starfire Beam (Recharge 5–6).** 60-ft. line, 5 ft. wide. Each
  creature in the line must make a DC 16 Dex save, taking 36 (8d8)
  radiant damage on a failure, half on success.

**REACTIONS**

- **Deflect.** When hit by a ranged attack, the Golem reduces the
  damage by 10 (1d10 + 5). If the damage is reduced to 0, the Golem
  catches or deflects the projectile.

**When Defeated:** The Golem kneels on one knee, its star dims, and
it presents the cracked star emblem to the victor with both hands.
The crowd erupts. This is the **Medal of the Seventh Star.**

| **STR** | 22 (+6) |
| **DEX** | 14 (+2) |
| **CON** | 18 (+4) |

---

### Phase 1 — (100% to 50% HP)

**ACTIONS**

- **Multiattack.** The Colossus makes two Rift Slam attacks.

- **Rift Slam.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one
  target. *Hit:* 19 (2d12 + 6) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) force
  damage.

- **Temporal Breath (Recharge 5–6).** 30-ft. cone. Each creature in
  the cone must make a DC 16 Con save:
  - Failed save: 28 (8d6) force damage and the creature is **Slowed**
    until the end of its next turn (speed halved, −2 AC, −2 Dex saves,
    can't use reactions).
  - Successful save: Half damage, no Slow effect.

---

### Phase 2 — (50% to 0% HP)

When the Colossus drops below 95 HP, it destabilizes:
- It gains **Rift Spawn** as a bonus action
- Its melee attacks deal an extra 7 (2d6) force damage (already
  reflected in Phase 2 damage below)
- Lightning crackles from the Rift more intensely

**MODIFIED ACTIONS**

- **Rift Slam (Empowered).** *Hit:* 19 (2d12 + 6) bludgeoning damage
  plus 14 (4d6) force damage.

- **Rift Spawn (Bonus Action).** The Colossus summons 2 **Rift
  Stalkers** within 15 ft. of itself. They act on the Colossus's
  initiative. Maximum 4 summoned Stalkers alive at any time.

---

### Lair Actions (While the Rift is Open)

On initiative count 20, the Rift discharges:

| d4 | Effect |
|----|--------|
| 1–2 | **Temporal Strike.** A bolt of energy hits a random 10-ft. square within 60 ft. DC 14 Dex save or 14 (4d6) force damage and cosmetically aged 10 years (reversed when Rift closes). |

## Valerius Thorne — The Philanthropist

**Custom | CR 12 | 8,400 XP**

> *This is the heroic version of Valerius — the man the world sees.
> He fights with restrained power, prioritizes civilian safety over
> damage, and uses his magic to protect rather than destroy.*

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **AC** | 18 (temporal distortion field) |
| **HP** | 150 (20d8 + 60) |
| **Speed** | 30 ft., fly 30 ft. (hover) |
| **STR** | 10 (+0) |
| **DEX** | 16 (+3) |
| **CON** | 16 (+3) |
| **INT** | 22 (+6) |
| **WIS** | 16 (+3) |
| **CHA** | 20 (+5) |

**Saving Throws:** Int +10, Wis +7, Con +7
**Skills:** Arcana +10, History +10, Insight +7, Persuasion +9
**Senses:** passive Perception 13
**Languages:** Common, Celestial, Draconic, Elvish, Sylvan

**Magic Resistance.** Advantage on saving throws against spells and
magical effects.

**Temporal Shield.** Valerius can use his reaction to grant an ally
within 30 ft. +5 AC against one attack. (He rewrites the moment — the
attacker's strike arrives a fraction of a second too late.)

**DM Guidance — Valerius in Combat:**
Valerius is an *enabler*, not a damage dealer. He:
- Uses Slow and Wall of Force to control the battlefield
- Uses Temporal Shield to protect the players
- Uses Chronal Bolt for consistent damage but never outshines the party
- Heals with Time Rewind if a PC is about to go down
- Protects civilians as his first priority (he will sacrifice a turn
  of offense to freeze a collapsing wall or shield a bystander)

**ACTIONS**

- **Chronal Bolt.** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +10 to hit, range 120 ft.,
  one target. *Hit:* 15 (2d8 + 6) force damage.

- **Spellcasting.** Spell save DC 18, +10 to hit.
  - *Slow* (3rd) — 120 ft. range, DC 18 Wis, up to 6 creatures
  - *Wall of Force* (5th) — 120 ft. range, 10 panels
  - *Counterspell* (at 5th level) — reaction, 60 ft.
  - *Shield* — reaction, +5 AC

- **Time Rewind (2/Day).** As a bonus action, Valerius targets one
  creature within 30 ft. that he can see. The target regains 22
  (4d8 + 4) hit points as Valerius rewinds their injuries. This looks
  like a golden glow flowing backward over their wounds.

**REACTIONS**

- **Temporal Shield.** (See above.)
- **Counterspell.** (See above.)
- **Shield.** (See above.)

**IMPORTANT: Valerius Must Not Steal the Show.**
- Limit him to 1 offensive action per round (Chronal Bolt or a spell)
- Spend his bonus actions on Time Rewind for PCs
- Use reactions to protect players, not himself
- Narrate his actions briefly between PC turns — don't give him long
  turns that slow down play
