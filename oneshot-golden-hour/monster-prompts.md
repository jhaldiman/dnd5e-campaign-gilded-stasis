# The Golden Hour — Monster & Creature Image Generation Guide

> **Workflow for Consistency:** 
> 1. **Apply the Style Anchor:** Use the same prefix as the character guide for a unified look.
> 2. **Review Visual Identifiers:** These descriptions match the creature stat blocks and descriptions in the module.
> 3. **Generate Portraits:** Use these prompts to create high-quality images for use as NPC portraits and token art.

---

## Part 1: The Style Anchor
*Use this prefix at the start of EVERY prompt to ensure monsters match the heroic/cinematic look of the characters.*

**Style Prefix:**
`Digital fantasy illustration, D&D fantasy art style, rich warm color palette with gold and deep blue tones, painterly lighting, detailed and polished, suitable for a tabletop RPG virtual tabletop display.`

---

## Part 2: Master Visual Identifiers
*Visual anchors for the creatures of Aurelius and the Temporal Rift.*

- **[CHIMERA]:** A smaller, "boutique" arena chimera. It has the head of a **regal lion**, a **black mountain goat**, and a **brass dragon**. It wears a **jeweled golden harness** that glows with soft enchantment magic.
- **[DRAKE]:** A muscular, wingless drake with **shimmering bronze scales**. It has a low, powerful profile and a heavy, spiked tail. It wears a decorative leather collar with gold studs.
- **[CHAMPION_GOLEM]:** A 10-foot-tall construct carved from **seamless white marble** in the likeness of a heroic gladiator. Its joints and eyes glow with **warm golden light**. It wields a massive **matching marble greatsword**.
- **[STAR_GOLEM]:** A magnificent 12-foot-tall construct made of **burnished gold and brass**. A blazing **seven-pointed star** is embedded in its chest. It carries a tower shield and a glowing golden greatsword.
- **[STALKER]:** A predatory creature of **shifting geometry and violet shadow**. Its form is sleek and feline, but its skin looks like **distorted star-charts**. It flickers in and out of reality, leaving faint afterimages.
- **[WRAITH]:** A spectral, translucent figure wreathed in **flickering chronal energy**. Its face and body rapidly shift between a **young youth, a middle-aged adult, and a withered skeleton**.
- **[COLOSSUS]:** A 15-foot-tall entity made of **shattered glass, obsidian, and crackling white lightning**. Its body follows "impossible" geometry, with floating limbs and a hollow chest where a miniature rift swirls.

## Part 3: Creature Portraits (Portraits 1–7)

**1. Arena Chimera Spawn**
> [Style Prefix] Full body portrait of **[CHIMERA]**. The creature is standing proudly in a sun-drenched arena. The jeweled harness sparkles in the light. Its three heads are looking in different directions with a majestic, trained demeanor.

**2. Arena Drake**
> [Style Prefix] Action portrait of **[DRAKE]**. The drake is prowling across a sandy arena floor, its bronze scales reflecting the bright sun. Its tail is mid-swipe, kicking up a small cloud of sand.

**3. Arena Champion Golem**
> [Style Prefix] Portrait of **[CHAMPION_GOLEM]**. The marble construct stands in a heroic "at ease" pose, its stone sword planted in the ground. Golden light leaks from the cracks in its joints. Cinematic lighting from above.

**4. Golem of the Seventh Star**
> [Style Prefix] Portrait of **[STAR_GOLEM]**. The golden construct is glowing with radiant energy. The seven-pointed star in its chest is a focal point of brilliant light. It holds its tower shield forward, looking like an unstoppable guardian.

**5. Rift Stalker**
> [Style Prefix] Portrait of **[STALKER]**. The creature is crouched on a city rooftop at night. Its form is partially transparent, revealing a field of stars "inside" its body. Violet lightning crackles around its paws.

**6. Temporal Wraith**
> [Style Prefix] Portrait of **[WRAITH]**. A haunting image where the face is a blurred composite of three ages. Chronal energy like heat-haze surrounds it. The background is a street where time seems to be frozen or melting.

**7. Riftborn Colossus**
> [Style Prefix] Portrait of **[COLOSSUS]**. A massive, terrifying entity of glass and lightning. It looms over a city street. Shards of its body float in the air around it, held by magnetic temporal forces. The air around it is distorted like a lens.

## Part 4: Technical Notes for Token Creation
*Once images are generated, use these tips for the best Foundry VTT tokens.*

- **Aspect Ratio:** Generate in 1:1 (Square) for easiest cropping.
- **Backgrounds:** The prompts include backgrounds for "flavor," but for tokens, you may want to use a background removal tool to make the creature "pop" against the map.
- **Colors:** If the Rift creatures (Stalker, Wraith, Colossus) look too similar, ask Gemini to "emphasize the violet and deep blue tones" for the Rift creatures to contrast with the "gold and white" of the Arena creatures.
