# The Golden Hour — Introductory One-Shot

> *A one-shot adventure for 4–5 level 10 characters, set 3 years before
> the events of The Gilded Stasis campaign.*

---

## Premise

The players are **The Silver Chalice**, a renowned adventuring party
invited to the **7th Grand Heroic Games** in the golden capital of
Aurelius. They have spent years making a name for themselves across the
Tri-Kingdoms, and the invitation from Archduke Valerius Thorne is the
highest honor a hero can receive.

This is a celebration. The city is beautiful, the people are kind, and
the Archduke is everything the stories say — brilliant, generous, and
genuinely devoted to making the world better. The players will compete
in the arena, befriend a rising adventuring party called the **Iron
Vanguard**, and fight alongside the Archduke himself when a crisis
threatens the capital.

**It is, by every measure, a perfect day.**

---

## The Secret (DM Only)

Everything in this one-shot is designed to make **Archduke Valerius
Thorne** look like the greatest leader the Tri-Kingdoms has ever known.
Every word he says is genuine. Every act of kindness is real. He is not
faking — he truly believes he is saving the world.

He is also, at this exact moment, three years from completing a ritual
that will freeze every living thing in the Tri-Kingdoms in a single
eternal moment. The Games are a harvesting operation. The "retirement
estates" are stasis pods. The heroes the players befriend today will be
frozen in glass within the year.

**Do not hint at any of this.** Play it completely straight. The goal is
for your players to genuinely like Valerius and the Iron Vanguard, so
that when the main campaign reveals the truth, it is devastating.

The horror of this one-shot only becomes apparent in hindsight.

| Heroes' Rest is a luxurious gift | It's a surveillance operation |

## Tone & Style

- **100% heroic fantasy.** This should feel like a victory lap, a
  celebration, a reward for being heroes.
- **Warm and funny.** The Iron Vanguard are at their best — Bron is
  boisterous, Torq is poetic, Pip is stealing everything, Sylara is
  quietly brilliant.
- **Valerius is the best version of himself.** He's witty, humble,
  personally courageous, and treats the players like honored friends.
  He remembers their names. He asks about their adventures. He laughs
  at their jokes.
- **No red flags.** The only "clues" are innocuous phrases that gain
  double meanings later. Do not play them with suspicion.

## One-Shot Structure (4–5 Hours)

| Act | Time | Summary |
|-----|------|---------|
| **Act 1 — Arrival** | 45 min | Arrive in Aurelius, meet Valerius and Elara, tour the city, settle into Heroes' Rest |
| **Act 2 — The Gauntlet** | 75 min | Arena event: the Gauntlet of Heroes (three-stage combat/puzzle challenge) |
| **Act 3 — The Gala** | 30 min | Victory celebration, meet the Iron Vanguard properly, Valerius's speech |
| **Act 4 — The Rift** | 75 min | Temporal rift tears open above the city; monsters pour through; fight alongside Valerius and the Vanguard |
| **Act 5 — Golden Hour** | 30 min | Aftermath, honors, the offer of "retirement," epilogue |

## Key NPCs in This One-Shot

### Archduke Valerius Thorne
- **Presentation:** Warm, witty, humble. Wears simple cream-and-gold
  robes. Silver-streaked dark hair. Walks among the people, not above
  them. Remembers everyone's name.
- **Key Moments:** Personally welcomes the players, watches their arena
  performance, fights alongside them during the Rift, and delivers a
  moving speech at the end.
- **Combat Role:** He uses time magic to save civilians (freezing
  collapsing buildings, slowing falling debris). He fights the rift
  creatures alongside the party, but his priority is always protecting
  the innocent.
- **Stat Block:** Use "Valerius — The Philanthropist" from stat-blocks.md.
  This is a restrained version — he's not going all-out.

### Herald Elara Dawnmantle
- **Presentation:** Earnest, kind, proud to serve. She genuinely
  believes in Valerius's mission. She's the party's guide and liaison.
- **Key Moment:** She heals wounded civilians during the Rift without
  hesitation.
- **Stat Block:** Use Phase 2 Elara (CR 9) from the main campaign.

### The Iron Vanguard
- **Where they are:** Also competing in the 7th Games. They haven't won
  yet — that's next year (the 8th). Right now they're the exciting
  underdogs. Crowd favorites.
- **Presentation:** At their absolute best. Full of life, full of hope.

| Member | Personality in the One-Shot |
|--------|-----------------------------|
| **Bron Ironfist** | Challenges the biggest PC to arm wrestling. Loses graciously. Buys everyone drinks. |
| **Sylara Moonwhisper** | Reads one PC's fortune (Portent). Gives a cryptically kind prediction. |
| **Torq** | Writes a terrible poem about the Silver Chalice on the spot. It's earnest and bad and wonderful. |
| **Pip Nimblefingers** | Steals a PC's coinpurse, returns it with extra coins inside "as a tip." |
