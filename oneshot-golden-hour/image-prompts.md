> `Digital fantasy illustration, D&D fantasy art style, rich warm color
> palette with gold and blue tones, painterly lighting, detailed and

## Usage Notes

- **Consistency tip:** If Gemini produces a version of a character you
  like, reference that image in subsequent scene prompts (e.g., "the
  same man in blue robes from the portrait") to maintain visual
  consistency.

- **Aspect ratios:** Use landscape (16:9) for scene images and
  portrait (3:4) for character portraits.

- **Batch generation:** Generate all NPC portraits first, then use
  those as reference for scene images featuring the same characters.

- **The Building Scene (#26)** is the single most important image in
  the one-shot. Consider generating multiple versions and picking the
  most dramatic one.

- **The Epilogue (#31)** works best as a wide, cinematic landscape
  with small figures — prioritize mood over character detail.
